Do not use global variables or your variables might get updated by someone else. Remember they are global. Use var to declare a variable. 

> var a = 2
> var b = 4
> a + b



Use sys.puts to print string. 

> sys.puts('hello world');
hello world




> function fn() {sys.puts('loving it')}
> fn()
loving it
